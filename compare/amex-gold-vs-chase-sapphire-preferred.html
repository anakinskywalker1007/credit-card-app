🏆 Amex Gold — If You Dine Out Often

The Amex Gold wins on dining (4X) and groceries (4X), plus $240 in annual credits almost covers the $325 fee. But the CSP has better transfer partners and a lower barrier to entry.

Pick Amex Gold if you spend heavily on restaurants and groceries. Pick CSP if you want flexibility and better travel partners.
