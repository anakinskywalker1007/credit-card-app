Credit Card vs Debit Card

Why you should use credit cards for purchases

💳 Credit Card

  • Earn rewards on every purchase
  • Build credit history
  • Purchase protection
  • Fraud protection (not liable)
  • Extend warranties
  • Track spending easily
  • No foreign fees (some cards)

🏧 Debit Card

  • Spend your own money
  • No annual fees
  • No debt risk
  • Limited protections
  • Direct bank access
  • May have foreign fees
  • No rewards (usually)

Key Benefits of Using Credit

1. Rewards: Earn 1-5% back on every purchase. Over a year, that's hundreds of dollars on normal spending.

2. Credit Building: Using credit responsibly builds your credit score, helping you get better loans, apartments, and even jobs.

3. Purchase Protection: Credit cards often offer protection if something goes wrong - extended warranties, fraud protection, disputed charges.

When to Use Debit

If you struggle with overspending or debt, debit cards help you stay within your means. Use what works for your financial situation.
