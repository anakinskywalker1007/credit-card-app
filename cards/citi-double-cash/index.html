Citi Double Cash Review 2026

The Citi Double Cash is the original "just give me cash back on everything" card, and nearly two decades after its launch, it still holds up as one of the strongest no-annual-fee options on the market. The premise is elegantly simple: earn 1% when you buy, 1% when you pay your bill. Every purchase. No rotating categories to activate, no spending caps, no category restrictions.

The effective 2% rate beats the Chase Freedom Unlimited's base rate of 1.5% on non-bonus purchases. If you're a high spender who puts everything on one card and doesn't want to manage multiple cards for different categories, the Citi Double Cash is mathematically optimal for your situation. At $50,000 in annual spending, you're earning $1,000 in cash back versus $750 from the Freedom Unlimited — a $250 annual difference.

The $200 welcome bonus requires $1,500 in spending over six months — a higher threshold than the Freedom Unlimited's $500 requirement, but still easily achievable. The longer timeframe is actually friendlier for those who don't spend heavily right away.

The 18-month 0% APR offer applies to balance transfers, not purchases — an important distinction. If you're carrying high-interest debt from another card, this is one of the better balance transfer offers available and can save hundreds in interest charges during the promotional period (note: a 3% balance transfer fee applies).

A lesser-known feature: the Citi Double Cash now earns Citi ThankYou Points rather than straight cash back. This matters because if you also hold a Citi Strata Premier or Strata Elite, those points become transferable to airline partners like JetBlue, Turkish Airlines, Singapore, and Air France/KLM. The Double Cash becomes a stealth travel rewards card — 2X ThankYou Points on everything, transferable to premium airline partners.

There are no travel insurance benefits, no lounge access, no credits. This is a pure spending card. But for what it does — simple, reliable 2% back on everything — it's genuinely excellent.

CardTier Bottom Line: The Citi Double Cash is the benchmark flat-rate cash back card. If you want maximum simplicity and the best return on general spending without paying an annual fee, this is the card. Pair it with a Citi Strata Premier to unlock transfer partner access and the value equation improves dramatically.
