Credit Card Fees to Avoid

Don't let fees eat your rewards

⚠️ Foreign Transaction Fee (3%)

Many cards charge 3% on purchases made abroad or with foreign merchants. Look for cards with NO foreign transaction fees if you travel or shop international sites.

⚠️ Annual Fee ($95-$695)

Annual fees are worth it IF the card's benefits exceed the cost. Example: $95 fee card with $200 annual credit = $105 net positive. Calculate before applying.

⚠️ Balance Transfer Fee (3-5%)

Most cards charge 3-5% to transfer a balance. Calculate if the savings from 0% APR exceed this fee.

⚠️ Cash Advance Fee (3-5%)

Avoid cash advances - fees are high (3-5%) and interest starts immediately with no grace period.

⚠️ Late Payment Fee ($25-$40)

Always pay on time! Set up autopay to avoid these completely. Late payments also hurt your credit score.

✅ Cards with NO Fees

No Annual Fee Cards

Citi Double Cash, Capital One Quicksilver, Discover it, Chase Freedom Unlimited - all have $0 annual fee.

No Foreign Transaction Fee Cards

Chase Sapphire cards, Capital One Venture, Amex cards - no fees on international purchases.
