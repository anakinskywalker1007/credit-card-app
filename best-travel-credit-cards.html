How We Pick the Best Travel Cards

Every card is ranked by net first-year value: the signup bonus (converted to dollars using The Points Guy's Feb 2026 valuations) plus annual credits, minus the annual fee. A card with a $695 fee but $900 in usable credits beats a no-fee card with a $200 bonus.

We also factor in transfer partners, lounge access, and foreign transaction fees — because a travel card that charges 3% abroad defeats the purpose.
