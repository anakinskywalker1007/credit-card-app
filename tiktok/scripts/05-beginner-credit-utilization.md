# Script 05: Credit Utilization — The Number Nobody Tells You About
**Pillar:** Beginner Explainer
**Target Length:** 35–45 seconds
**Target Audience:** 18–24, has a card but not sure how to use it

---

## HOOK (0:00–0:02)
**Spoken:** "There's a number on your credit report that almost nobody talks about, and it might be tanking your score right now."
**On-screen text:** 🚨 This number is quietly wrecking your credit score

---

## BODY (0:02–0:38)

**[Beat 1 — What it is]**
**Spoken:** "It's called credit utilization. It's the percentage of your total credit limit that you're currently using. And it makes up 30% of your FICO score."
**On-screen text:** Credit Utilization = Balance ÷ Credit Limit | Worth 30% of your score

**[Beat 2 — The math]**
**Spoken:** "Example: your card has a $1,000 limit. You spend $400 on it. That's 40% utilization. FICO wants to see you under 30% — ideally under 10%."
**On-screen text:** $400 spent on $1,000 limit = 40% ❌ | Aim for under 30% ✅ | Under 10% = elite 🏆

**[Beat 3 — The trick]**
**Spoken:** "Here's the trick nobody tells you: pay your card BEFORE the statement closes, not just before the due date. The balance that reports to the bureaus is your statement balance, not what you pay."
**On-screen text:** 💡 Pay BEFORE statement closes — not just by due date

**[Beat 4 — Quick fix]**
**Spoken:** "Or ask for a credit limit increase. Same spending, higher limit, lower percentage. Instant score boost."
**On-screen text:** 🔑 Request a limit increase → Same spending → Lower % → Higher score
