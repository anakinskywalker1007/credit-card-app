## 🎯 Brand Voice & Tone

| **Do** | **Don't** |
|--------|-----------|
| Be direct — say the thing | Hedge everything ("it depends...") |
| Have opinions ("This card is trash") | Sound like a bank brochure |
| Use Gen Z/Millennial language naturally | Try too hard to be "hip" |
| Make finance feel empowering | Make finance feel scary or exclusive |
| Use real numbers and math | Vague claims without backup |
| Be fast — no wasted seconds | Long intros or "hey guys welcome back" |
| Credit brands by name | Avoid naming cards (it kills shareability) |

### Personality Archetype
**The Knowledgeable Friend** — You're the person in the friend group who actually understands money. You're not a financial advisor (don't say you are). You're someone who did the research so your audience doesn't have to. 

Think: Frank, confident, occasionally funny about how absurd finance can be.

## 📐 Content Pillars (4 Pillars)

### Pillar 1: Tier Lists 🏆 (30% of content)
**Purpose:** Highest viral ceiling. Tier lists are TikTok crack — they're inherently engaging, shareable, and drive comments from people who disagree.

**Format:**
- On-screen tier chart graphic (S/A/B/C/D/F)
- Fast pacing — max 10 seconds per tier
- End on a spicy take to drive comments

**Topics to rotate through:**
- Travel cards tier list
- Cash back cards tier list
- Best first cards tier list
- Business cards tier list
- Hotel cards tier list
- Cards for bad credit tier list
- "Hidden gem" cards tier list

### Pillar 2: Beginner Explainers 📚 (25% of content)
**Purpose:** Build trust with the 18–25 audience who's just starting out. These become evergreen search traffic over time.

**Format:**
- "Explained in X seconds" hook
- Use on-screen callouts for key numbers
- No jargon without definition
- End with an actionable takeaway

**Topics to rotate through:**
- Credit score explained
- Credit utilization
- Annual fees — worth it or not?
- APR explained
- Hard vs soft pulls
- How to get your first card
- What happens if you miss a payment
- Credit limit increases

### Pillar 3: "Did You Know" 🤯 (25% of content)
**Purpose:** Broad reach, high shareability. These are designed to create the "wait, WHAT?" reaction.

**Format:**
- Start with "Did you know..." or similar myth-buster hook
- Short and punchy — 30–40 seconds max
- Designed to be saved and shared

**Topics to rotate through:**
- Soft vs hard pulls
- Sign-up bonuses are free money
- Paying before statement date trick
- Authorized users hack for credit building
- Airline miles don't expire (most of them)
- Foreign transaction fees — how to avoid
- Price protection/purchase protection features nobody uses

### Pillar 4: Rewards Optimization 💎 (20% of content)
**Purpose:** Serve the power-user audience (26–35). These videos position CardTier as the authority for serious rewards maximization.

**Format:**
- More detail OK (up to 60 seconds)
- Introduce card ecosystems (Chase, Amex, etc.)
- Include transfer partners, point valuations
- Power audience will share these

**Topics:**
- Chase Trifecta
- Amex Gold optimization
- Hotel points vs airline miles — which is better
- How to find award availability
- Best transfer partners
- 5/24 rule explained
- Category stacking strategies

## 🎬 Video Production Guidelines

### Format
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical, full screen
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 minimum
- **Length:** 30–60 seconds (sweet spot: 45–55 sec)
- **Caption style:** Always add on-screen text — 40% of TikTok is watched without sound

### On-Screen Text Rules
- Max 5–7 words per text card
- High-contrast font (white text, dark shadow or background bar)
- Bold sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat Bold, DIN, or TikTok's native fonts)
- Keep key numbers large — people screenshot these
- Arrows, emoji, and callout boxes increase watch time

### Filming Style Options (pick your lane early)
1. **Talking head** — Presenter on camera, clean background, text overlays. Most authentic. Builds personal brand.
2. **Screen recording / graphic format** — For tier list videos specifically. Use a visual tier chart.
3. **B-roll + voiceover** — Travel/lifestyle footage with voiceover. Works well for rewards content.

### Hook Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- First 1.5 seconds are make-or-break
- Never start with "Hey guys" or "So today I want to talk about"
- Start with the most interesting claim, contrarian take, or specific number
- On-screen text hook should appear in the FIRST FRAME

### Pacing
- One new piece of information every 4–6 seconds
- Cuts on-beat with music
- No dead air — if you're not saying something, cut it

## 🚫 Content Rules / Legal Notes

1. **Not financial advice disclaimer** — Add "not financial advice" in description on any video making specific recommendations
2. **Don't guarantee specific reward values** — Say "up to $600" or "worth around..." — values fluctuate
3. **Accurate rates** — Don't hard-code APRs into scripts; they change. Always say "check current rates at cardtier.com"
4. **FTC compliance** — If any video is ever sponsored by a card issuer, disclose clearly (#ad, #sponsored, #partner)
5. **No guarantees on approval odds** — Say "you may qualify" not "you will get approved"
