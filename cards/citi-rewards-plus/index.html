Citi Rewards+ Review 2026

The Citi Rewards+ is a cash back card from Citi that earns 2X on Gas/Groceries. Rounds up every purchase to nearest 10 points.

The signup bonus of 15K pts is valued at approximately $285, requiring $1,000 in spending within 3 mo. With no annual fee, this card provides strong value with zero financial commitment.

Earning rates are tiered: 2X on Gas/Groceries, 1X on Everything. This structure rewards small bonus effectively.

The card includes Round Up to Nearest 10 Points, 10% Points Back on First 100K, No Annual Fee.

Points are transferable to Citi ThankYou — unlocking premium travel redemption values beyond standard cash back rates.

CardTier Bottom Line: The Citi Rewards+ earns a C-Tier rating with $285 in first-year value. Best suited for: Small bonus. Note: this card charges foreign transaction fees, so use a different card abroad.
