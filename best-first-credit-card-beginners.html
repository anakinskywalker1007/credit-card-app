💡 Tips for Your First Card

  • Always pay on time - Payment history is 35% of your credit score
  • Keep utilization under 30% - Don't max out your card
  • Don't apply for too many at once - Each application hurts your score
  • Start with one card - Build history before adding more
  • Check your credit score - Use Credit Karma or similar for free
