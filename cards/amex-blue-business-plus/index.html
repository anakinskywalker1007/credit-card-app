Amex Blue Business Plus® Review 2026

The Amex Blue Business Plus® is a business card from Amex that earns 2X on Everything (up to $50K/year). 2X on all business purchases with no annual fee.

The signup bonus of 15K pts is valued at approximately $300, requiring $3,000 in spending within 3 mo. With no annual fee, this card is one of the best value business cards available.

The flat 2X earning rate on all purchases (up to $50,000 per year) then 1X makes it simple and effective for small businesses.

Notable perks: 2X on Everything (up to $50K), 0% APR 12 months, No Annual Fee. Transferable Membership Rewards points to Delta, Marriott, Hilton and more.

CardTier Bottom Line: The Amex Blue Business Plus® earns a B-Tier rating with $300 in first-year value. Best suited for: Business flat rate. The combination of no fee + 2X + transfer partners is hard to beat.
