## THE FORMULA

1. **Hook = the perk** (not the card name)
2. **Story = you discovering it** (not explaining it)
3. **Tone = disbelief** ("why doesn't everyone know this")
4. **CTA = none or very subtle** ("I have a breakdown on my site" only if it fits)

No "sign up with my link." No "check out cardtier.com." Just... perks that make people go "wait what"
