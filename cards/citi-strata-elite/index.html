Citi Strata Elite Review 2026

The Citi Strata Elite is a travel rewards card from Citi that earns 3X on Travel and Dining. Top transfer partners for serious points collectors.

The signup bonus of 100K pts is valued at approximately $1,900, requiring $4,000 in spending within 3 mo. The annual fee is $95, waived the first year.

Earning rates: 3X on Travel, 3X on Dining. This structure rewards frequent travelers and diners effectively.

Notable perks: $300 Air Travel Credit, $100 Hotel Credit, 10 Transfer Partners. No foreign transaction fees.

Points transferable to JetBlue, Singapore, Turkish, Virgin Atlantic, Cathay Pacific — excellent for international travel.

CardTier Bottom Line: The Citi Strata Elite earns a A-Tier rating with $1,900 in first-year value. Best for: Flexible travel. Strong transfer partner lineup makes this card special for points enthusiasts.
