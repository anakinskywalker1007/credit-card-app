# Script 08: Did You Know — Hard vs Soft Credit Pulls
**Pillar:** "Did You Know" Hook
**Target Length:** 30–40 seconds
**Target Audience:** Broad reach — anyone who's ever been scared to check their credit or apply for a card

---

## HOOK (0:00–0:02)
**Spoken:** "Did you know checking your own credit score does NOT hurt it? And most people don't know this."
**On-screen text:** 🤯 Checking your credit score does NOT hurt it. (Most people don't know this)

---

## BODY (0:02–0:32)

**[Beat 1 — The two types]**
**Spoken:** "There are two types of credit checks. A soft pull — that's when YOU check your score, or when a company pre-screens you for an offer. Doesn't affect your score at all."
**On-screen text:** SOFT PULL = You checking your score, pre-approvals → NO impact ✅

**[Beat 2 — Hard pull]**
**Spoken:** "A hard pull is when you formally APPLY for credit — a new card, a loan, a mortgage. That can ding your score 5 to 10 points. Temporarily. It recovers in 6 to 12 months."
**On-screen text:** HARD PULL = Applying for credit → -5 to -10 points (temporary, recovers in 6–12 months)

**[Beat 3 — The actionable stuff]**
**Spoken:** "So check your score for free right now. Credit Karma, your bank app, most credit cards show it free. And if you're rate shopping — like multiple mortgage lenders in two weeks — those all count as ONE hard pull. The system isn't totally evil."
**On-screen text:** 
✅ Check free: Credit Karma / your bank app
💡 Rate shopping in 14 days = counted as 1 hard pull

---

## CTA (0:32–0:42)
**Spoken:** "If you want to see which cards you pre-qualify for with zero hard pull, CardTier dot com has a filter for exactly that."
**On-screen text:** 🔍 See Pre-Qualified Offers (No Hard Pull) → CardTier.com

---

## On-Screen Text Summary
1. "Checking your credit score does NOT hurt it"
2. "SOFT PULL → NO impact ✅"
3. "HARD PULL → -5 to -10 pts (recovers in 6–12 months)"
4. "Rate shopping in 14 days = 1 hard pull 💡"
5. "Pre-Qualified Offers (No Hard Pull) → CardTier.com"

Satisfying "reveal" audio — dramatic pause + beat drop when the myth is busted. Clean instrumental works well.
