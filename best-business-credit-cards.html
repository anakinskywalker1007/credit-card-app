Choosing the Right Business Card

The best business card depends on your spend mix. High travel spend? Chase Ink Business Preferred or Amex Business Platinum. Everything else? Spark Cash Plus at a flat 2% is unbeatable. Startup with no personal guarantee required? Brex 70 is purpose-built for you.

Key difference from personal cards: business cards typically have higher spending limits, better expense tracking, and free employee cards. The signup bonuses are also larger — the Amex Business Platinum offers 250K points ($5,000 value) right now.
