Amex Platinum® Review 2026

The Amex Platinum® is the gold standard of premium travel credit cards, offering unmatched lounge access and a suite of travel credits that can offset the hefty annual fee.

The signup bonus of 175K pts is valued at approximately $3,500, requiring $12,000 in spending within 6 months. The annual fee is $895 — significant, but the $200 Uber Cash, $200 airline fee credit, and other credits can bring the net cost far lower.

Earning rates: 5X on Flights, 5X on Hotels booked through Amex Travel.

Notable perks include: Centurion Lounge Access, $200 Uber Cash, $200 Airline Fee Credit, Priority Pass Select, Hilton Honors Gold Status, Marriott Bonvoy Gold. No foreign transaction fees.

Points are transferable to Delta, Marriott, Hilton, British Airways, Singapore, Emirates — some of the best transfer partners in the game.

CardTier Bottom Line: The Amex Platinum® earns a S-Tier rating with $3,500 in first-year value. Best for: Maximum perks. If you travel frequently and use the credits, this card pays for itself many times over.
