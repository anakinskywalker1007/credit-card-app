How to Get Maximum Value

1. Transfer to Partners: Usually gives best value. Example: Amex points transfer to Delta at 1:1.

2. Book Through Portal: Chase UR portal values at 1.25-1.5 cents per point.

3. Statement Credits: Usually only worth 1 cent or less.

4. Gift Cards: Usually 0.8-1 cent - often not best value.

Pro Tips

Transfer bonuses can boost value 30-50%. Example: 30% bonus on Amex to Hilton = 1.3 cents per point instead of 0.5 cents.
