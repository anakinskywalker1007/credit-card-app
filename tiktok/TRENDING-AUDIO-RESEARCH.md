## 🎬 Trending Content FORMATS (Beyond Audio)

### Format 1: The "Real vs Expectation" Split Screen
**What it is:** Left side shows the "what people think" scenario, right side shows reality.
**CardTier use:** Left: "What people think building credit looks like" (complex graph, stress). Right: "What it actually takes" (coffee + credit card + pay in full).

### Format 2: Text-on-Screen Carousel / Slideshow
**What it is:** Series of text cards with minimal or no spoken word — designed for silent viewing.
**CardTier use:** "5 credit card mistakes ranked by how much they cost you" — pure text cards, trending instrumental.

### Format 3: The "Stitch with Hot Take" Response
**What it is:** Stitching a bad financial take from another creator and correcting it.
**CardTier use:** Find a viral TikTok with wrong credit card advice → Stitch it → "Actually, here's what that card is really worth."

### Format 4: "Things no one tells you about X"
**What it is:** Fast list format, no fluff, delivered like insider knowledge.
**CardTier use:** "Things no one tells you about your first credit card" — 5 bullets, delivered fast.

### Format 5: The Green Screen Behind Stats / Screenshots
**What it is:** Creator in front of a screenshot, chart, or graphic using green screen.
**CardTier use:** Stand in front of a CardTier.com comparison screenshot, walk through the rankings in real-time.

## 🔍 How to Stay Current on Trending Audio

1. **TokChart.com** — Updates daily with top trending TikTok sounds
2. **TikTok Creator Search Insights** (in TikTok app) — Shows trending audio in your niche
3. **Dash Social Trending Sounds Blog** — Monthly round-up, business-focused (dashsocial.com/blog/tiktok-sounds)
4. **HeyOrca** — Weekly updated trending audio list (heyorca.com/blog/trending-audio-for-reels-tiktok)
5. **The "For You Page" Method** — Open a secondary account, train it with personal finance content for 1 week, then note which audios keep appearing in the creator's suggested sounds panel

**Rule of thumb:** Use a sound when it's at 500K–3M videos. Under 500K = too early (lower reach), over 10M = past peak.
