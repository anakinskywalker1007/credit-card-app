# Script 02: Cash Back Credit Cards Tier List
**Pillar:** Tier List
**Target Length:** 45–55 seconds
**Target Audience:** 18–30, cash back curious, any income level

---

## HOOK (0:00–0:02)
**Spoken:** "POV: you're getting 5% cash back while your friend has a card that gives them 0.5%. Let me explain the difference."
**On-screen text:** 💸 CASH BACK TIER LIST — Who wins? 💸

---

## BODY (0:02–0:45)

**[Beat 1 — S Tier]**
**Spoken:** "S-tier has to be the Citi Double Cash. 2% on literally everything — no categories to track, no activation buttons to click, no annual fee. Set it and forget it."
**On-screen text:** 💳 Citi Double Cash → S TIER (2% everything)

**[Beat 2 — S Tier also]**
**Spoken:** "Also S-tier: Discover it Cash Back. First year they MATCH every dollar you earn. That's up to 10% your first year. Insane."
**On-screen text:** 🔥 Discover it → S TIER (First-year match!)

**[Beat 3 — B Tier]**
**Spoken:** "B-tier: the Chase Freedom Flex. 5% rotating categories — good if you remember to activate it. Most people forget. Hence B."
**On-screen text:** 📅 Chase Freedom Flex → B TIER (Don't forget to activate!)

**[Beat 4 — C Tier]**
**Spoken:** "C-tier: store cards — Target RedCard, Amazon Prime. Great if you only shop there. Terrible if you have a life."
**On-screen text:** 🛒 Store Cards → C TIER (Too limited)

**[Beat 5 — D/F Tier]**
**Spoken:** "F-tier: cards advertising 1% cash back with a $95 annual fee and a 28% APR. You're literally paying them to earn less money than a savings account."
**On-screen text:** ❌ 1% + $95 fee + 28% APR → F TIER 🗑️
