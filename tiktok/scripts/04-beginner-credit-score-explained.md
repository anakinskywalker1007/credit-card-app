# Script 04: What Is a Credit Score? (Beginner Explainer)
**Pillar:** Beginner Explainer
**Target Length:** 45–60 seconds
**Target Audience:** 18–25, first encounter with adult finance, Gen Z

---

## HOOK (0:00–0:02)
**Spoken:** "Your credit score is literally just a grade — and nobody taught you how it's graded."
**On-screen text:** 📊 CREDIT SCORE EXPLAINED in 60 seconds

---

## BODY (0:02–0:50)

**[Beat 1 — What it is]**
**Spoken:** "Your credit score is a number between 300 and 850. It tells lenders — banks, landlords, even some employers — how risky you are to lend money to. Higher number, better deals. Lower number, you get punished with higher interest rates."
**On-screen text:** 300–850 | Higher = Better Deals | Lower = You Pay More

**[Beat 2 — The 5 factors]**
**Spoken:** "Here's exactly how it's calculated. 35% is payment history — pay on time, this is your biggest lever. 30% is how much of your credit limit you're using — keep it under 30%. 15% is how long you've had credit — which is why you should open your first card ASAP. 10% is new applications. 10% is credit mix."
**On-screen text:** 
- 35% — Pay on time 🏆
- 30% — Keep usage under 30%
- 15% — Length of history (open early!)
- 10% — New applications
- 10% — Credit mix

**[Beat 3 — The fast hack]**
**Spoken:** "The fastest way to build your score: get a credit card, buy one thing a month — like a coffee — and pay it off IN FULL before the due date. That's it. Six months of that and you'll have a real score."
**On-screen text:** ☕ Buy coffee → Pay in full → Wait 6 months → Real credit score

**[Beat 4 — The trap]**
**Spoken:** "The trap people fall into: they think carrying a balance helps. It does NOT. Carrying a balance just means you're paying interest for zero benefit."
**On-screen text:** 🚨 Myth: Carrying a balance builds credit. WRONG. It just costs you money.
