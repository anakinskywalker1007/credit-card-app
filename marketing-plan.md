## Phase 3: Social & Community (Week 2-3)

### Platforms
1. **Reddit** - r/creditcards, r/churning, r/personalfinance
   - Answer questions, provide value, link to relevant pages
2. **Twitter/X** - Share card deals, bonuses
3. **YouTube** - Card reviews, comparison videos
4. **TikTok** - Quick credit card tips

### Strategy
- Provide genuine value first
- Don't spam - be helpful member of community
- Link to relevant CardTier pages when appropriate
