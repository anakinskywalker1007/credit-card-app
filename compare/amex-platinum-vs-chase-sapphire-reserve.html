🏆 Chase Sapphire Reserve — For Most People

The CSR has a lower fee, simpler rewards structure, and better transfer partners (Hyatt). The Amex Platinum has more credits but you need to use them all to justify $695.

Get CSR if you want premium benefits without the complexity. Get Amex Platinum if you'll max out every credit and value lounge access.
