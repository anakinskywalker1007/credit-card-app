## 1. CREDIT KARMA

### Overview
Credit Karma is the largest free credit monitoring platform in the US (~120M members). Now owned by Intuit (TurboTax). The affiliate program is focused on driving **new member signups** — the service is free, which means conversion rates are unusually high.

### Commission Structure

| Action | Commission | Network |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| New member signup | **$2.00** per signup | Impact Radius |
| New member signup | **$6.00** per signup | Wecantrack/various |
| Credit Karma Money — Direct Deposit setup ($750+/mo) | **$25.00** per account | Awin (merchant #70911) |
| General signup (US) | Varies | Awin (merchant #66532) |

**Notes:**
- Rates vary significantly by network — the Awin Credit Karma Money program ($25 CPA) is the highest-value play
- Some sources cite $0.25/signup for low-tier traffic networks (diggitymarketing.com)
- Commission rates for financial affiliates are often negotiated up with volume — starting rates are typically the floor

### Networks to Join
1. **Impact Radius** (impact.com) — Primary network, most publishers
2. **Awin** (awin.com) — Separate programs for Credit Karma Money (higher commission) and general
3. **FlexOffers** — Good for new affiliates, easier approval

### Signup Requirements
- No official minimum traffic requirement stated
- Finance niche sites strongly preferred
- Website required (no pure social media applications typically approved)
- Must disclose affiliate relationship per FTC guidelines
- Application reviewed manually — expect 2–5 business days on Impact/Awin

### Approval Process
1. Sign up for chosen network (Impact or Awin)
2. Apply specifically to Credit Karma program within the network
3. Fill out publisher profile — **detail your finance site/audience clearly**
4. Wait for manual approval (typical: 2–5 business days)
5. For Awin specifically: $5 deposit required (refunded if rejected)

### Strategy Notes for CardTier
- High conversion (free product = low barrier) makes this volume play
- Best placement: credit monitoring articles, "how to check your credit score" content
- The $25 Credit Karma Money program is the real prize — worth separate content targeting bank account switchers
- Cookie duration: typically 30 days

## 2. EXPERIAN

### Overview
One of the three major credit bureaus. Offers consumer products including credit monitoring (Experian CreditWorks), identity theft protection, and credit score services (free tier + $24.99/mo premium). Higher purchase intent than Credit Karma — users often searching to fix specific credit issues.

### Commission Structure

| Product | Commission | Network |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| CreditWorks Basic (free) | **$8** per signup | CJ Affiliate |
| CreditWorks Premium trial | **$30 CPA** | CJ Affiliate / direct |
| IdentityWorks subscription | **$8–$12** per sale | Various |
| Combined/Tiered | **$6–$30** depending on product | CJ / Impact / Awin |

**Notes:**
- $30 CPA is for converting free-trial users to paid subscribers — requires deeper funnel
- $8/signup is the consistent base rate across most networks
- Some sources cite 6.6% revenue share as an alternative structure (37x.com aggregator)
- CJ Affiliate appears to be the primary and most reliable network

### Networks to Join
1. **CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)** — Primary and highest-volume network for Experian
2. **Impact Radius** — Secondary network
3. **Awin** — Available, slightly lower rates

### Signup Requirements
- Finance/credit niche strongly preferred — generic content sites may be rejected
- Active site with established content (at least 10–20 published articles recommended)
- Privacy Policy page required — ✅ (we've now created this)
- Terms of Service page recommended
- CJ Affiliate account required (create at cj.com)
- No minimum traffic stated, but thin sites get rejected

### Approval Process
1. Create CJ Affiliate account at **app.cj.com**
2. Complete publisher profile with website details (enter cardtier.com)
3. Search for "Experian" in the Advertisers directory
4. Click "Apply to Program" — approval usually within 24–72 hours
5. Once approved, generate tracking links and implement on site

### Strategy Notes for CardTier
- Best fit for: "how to check your credit score," credit monitoring comparison content, "what's in my credit report" articles
- The $30 CPA for Premium conversions is strong — worth creating a dedicated "Experian vs Credit Karma vs Credit Sesame" comparison page
- Experian has strong brand trust, conversion rates tend to be solid
- **Priority application** — highest CPA of the three options here

## 3. CREDIT SESAME

### Overview
Credit Sesame is a credit monitoring and personal finance platform offering free credit scores, credit monitoring, and a banking product (Sesame Cash). It has its own affiliate program accessible both directly and through networks. More flexible than Credit Karma with payout model options.

### Commission Structure

| Model | Rate | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) | **$3–$6** per signup | Most common |
| Revenue Share | Variable | Available for high-volume partners |
| Hybrid | CPA + RevShare | Available on request |
| CPA (current spot market) | **$1.29** | Per affplus.com (low-tier traffic) |

**Notes:**
- Credit Sesame's own affiliate page (creditsesame.com/affiliate-program/) offers CPA, revenue share, or hybrid
- They run their own program AND are on FlexOffers and CommissionSoup
- The direct program likely offers best rates for an established site
- Service is 100% free = very high conversion rates

### Networks to Join (in priority order)
1. **Direct Program** — creditsesame.com/affiliate-program/ — likely best rates
2. **FlexOffers** — flexoffers.com — easy approval, good support
3. **CommissionSoup** — commissionsoup.com — specialized in credit/finance

### Signup Requirements
- Finance/credit niche site
- No explicit minimum traffic requirement
- Privacy policy required ✅
- Application through affiliate page or network

### Approval Process — Direct Program
1. Visit **creditsesame.com/affiliate-program/**
2. Fill out the affiliate application form
3. Include: site URL, monthly traffic estimate, audience description, how you plan to promote
4. Typical review: 3–7 business days
5. Once approved, receive custom tracking links

### Approval Process — FlexOffers
1. Create publisher account at **flexoffers.com**
2. Search for "Credit Sesame" and apply
3. FlexOffers is known for faster approvals (often same-day to 48 hrs)

### Strategy Notes for CardTier
- Lower CPA than Experian but very easy to convert (free product)
- Best used in: credit score monitoring articles, comparison content
- The "Experian vs Credit Karma vs Credit Sesame" comparison page could serve all three affiliate programs simultaneously — high-value content play
- Consider testing direct vs. network to identify which converts better for your traffic
