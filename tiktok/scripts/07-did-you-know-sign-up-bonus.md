# Script 07: Did You Know — Sign-Up Bonuses Are Free Money
**Pillar:** "Did You Know" Hook
**Target Length:** 30–40 seconds
**Target Audience:** Everyone (broad reach), 18–40

---

## HOOK (0:00–0:02)
**Spoken:** "Did you know credit card sign-up bonuses are essentially free money — and most people leave $500 to $1,000 on the table every year?"
**On-screen text:** 🤑 Did You Know? You're leaving $500–$1,000/year on the table

---

## BODY (0:02–0:32)

**[Beat 1 — The basics]**
**Spoken:** "Banks offer sign-up bonuses to get you to open a new card. Typical offer: spend $3,000 in three months, get 60,000 points. Those points are worth around $600 to $900 in travel or cash back."
**On-screen text:** Spend $3K in 3 months → Get 60,000 points → Worth $600–$900 ✈️💸

**[Beat 2 — The key insight]**
**Spoken:** "Here's the key: you're not spending extra money. You're spending money you'd already spend — groceries, gas, rent if your landlord takes cards — on a new card instead of your old one. Same spending. Free $600."
**On-screen text:** ✅ Same spending on a NEW card = Free $600
(You're already spending the money!)

**[Beat 3 — The one rule]**
**Spoken:** "The one rule: NEVER carry a balance. The interest will eat your bonus alive. Pay it off every month. Full stop."
**On-screen text:** ⚠️ Golden Rule: Pay in FULL every month or the interest kills the bonus
