Flat Rate vs. Category Cards

Flat-rate cards (like Citi Double Cash at 2% on everything) win if your spending is varied and you hate tracking categories. Category cards (like Capital One SavorCash at 4% dining) win if you spend heavily in specific areas. The best strategy: pair one flat-rate card with one strong category card.
